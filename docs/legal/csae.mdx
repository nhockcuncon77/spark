# Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Standards

Blindly has **zero tolerance** for any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

Protecting minors is a fundamental responsibility. Any behavior, content, or activity that exploits, abuses, or endangers children is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action.

---

## Definition of CSAE

CSAE (Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation) refers to any content, behavior, or activity that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers a minor (any person under 18 years of age).

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Sexual abuse of a child  
- Sexual exploitation of a child  
- Grooming a minor for sexual purposes  
- Sextortion involving a minor  
- Trafficking or facilitating trafficking of a child for sexual purposes  
- Creating, sharing, requesting, or possessing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)  
- Any attempt to engage a minor in sexual conversation or activity  

---

## Blindly’s Zero-Tolerance Policy

Blindly strictly prohibits:

- Any sexual content involving minors  
- Any attempts to contact, groom, or manipulate minors  
- Any use of the platform to exploit or endanger children  
- Any sharing, storage, or transmission of CSAM  

Violations result in **immediate and permanent account termination**, without warning.

---

## Age Restrictions

- Blindly is strictly limited to users **18 years and older**.
- We do not knowingly allow minors on the platform.
- Any account suspected of belonging to a minor will be suspended pending verification.

---

## Prevention & Detection Measures

Blindly actively works to prevent CSAE through:

- Mandatory age verification during onboarding  
- Government ID verification and liveness checks  
- Automated detection systems for suspicious behavior  
- Behavioral monitoring for grooming patterns  
- Manual review by trained moderators  
- Reporting and escalation workflows  

---

## Reporting CSAE

Users can report suspected CSAE directly through:

- In-app reporting tools  
- Contacting Blindly support  

All CSAE-related reports are treated as **high priority**.

---

## Cooperation With Authorities

Blindly fully cooperates with law enforcement and relevant authorities when CSAE is suspected.

This includes:

- Preserving relevant data  
- Reporting confirmed CSAE cases to appropriate agencies  
- Assisting investigations as required by law  

---

## Consequences of Violations

Any involvement in CSAE will result in:

- Immediate account termination  
- Permanent ban from the platform  
- Reporting to law enforcement where required  
- Blocking of associated devices and identifiers  

---

## Commitment to Safety

Blindly is committed to:

- Maintaining a safe environment for all users  
- Continuously improving detection and prevention systems  
- Following applicable child protection laws and regulations  
- Acting swiftly and responsibly when CSAE risks are identified
