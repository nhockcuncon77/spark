# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: December 2025_

Blindly is designed to protect what matters most: your identity, your autonomy, and your right to control your own data.  
This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

---

## Summary

- We **never** show your photos until both sides choose to reveal.  
- Your identity verification data is **encrypted and never shared** with other users.  
- AI features are **transparent, optional, and clearly labeled**.  
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.  

---

# 1. What We Collect

Blindly collects only the information required to provide a safe, functional, and personalized dating experience.

## 1.1 Account Information
We collect:
- Name (from ID verification)
- Phone number or email (for login)
- Date of birth (to verify age)
- Gender & preferences
- Photos (hidden and encrypted)

## 1.2 Profile Data
- Hobbies and interests  
- Personality responses  
- Bio (user-written or AI-assisted)  
- Profile themes  
- Privacy settings  

## 1.3 Identity Verification
To ensure a safe environment, Blindly requires:
- Government ID
- Liveness selfie
- Device metadata used to prevent fraud

These files are encrypted at rest and never shown to other users.

## 1.4 Behavioral & Usage Data
Used to improve recommendations and detect spam:
- Chat engagement patterns (message length, reciprocity, not content of private chats for training)
- Feature usage events
- Anonymous analytics (app performance, crashes, device type)

## 1.5 Social Feed Content
Posts, likes, comments, and pokes.  
Your profile identity is hidden; only your hobby badges, personality headline, and theme are visible.

## 1.6 AI Interaction Data
When using AI tools:
- Prompts (bio generator, rizz suggestions)
- Uploaded photos for AI editing  
- Feedback signals  

We **do not** use this data to train public models.  
Your inputs remain private to your account and Blindly’s internal models.

## 2.1 Core App Functionality
- Creating your profile

## 2.2 Safety & Moderation

# 3. Data Sharing

Blindly **does not sell or rent** your data.  
We only share what is necessary:

## 3.1 Third-Party Service Providers
- Identity verification partners  
- Cloud storage providers  
- Analytics tools  
- Payment processors (if applicable)

All partners follow strict compliance and contractual obligations.

## 3.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose data if required to:
- Comply with law  
- Respond to valid legal requests  
- Prevent fraud or harm  

---

# 4. Photo Privacy & Reveal System

Blindly’s signature feature is its **mutual unlock system**.

- Photos stay encrypted until *both* users accept reveal.  
- If one user declines, no images are shared.  
- Once revealed, users can rate the match; ratings are private.  
- Revealed photos are never shown to anyone else or reused for public training.

---

# 5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed:

- Profile data: retained until account deletion  
- Verification data: minimal required duration for safety  
- Photos: fully removed upon deletion  
- Chat messages: deleted automatically when a match ends or a user deletes their account  
- AI-generated data: cleared with account deletion  

You may request permanent deletion at any time.

---

# 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

- **Access**. Request a copy of your data.  
- **Correction**. Fix incorrect information.  
- **Deletion**. Delete your account and all related data.  
- **Consent Withdrawal**. Disable AI features or revoke permissions.  
- **Portability**. Export your data.  

Use the in-app support page to submit requests.

---

# 7. Children’s Privacy

Blindly is strictly for users **18 years and older**.  
We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

# 8. Security

We employ:
- Encrypted storage for photos and IDs  
- Zero-trust architecture for sensitive data  
- Rate limiting and bot detection  
- Secure access controls  
- Periodic audits  

No system is perfect, but Blindly is built with security as a core foundation.

We follow GDPR-style data principles globally, even where not legally required.
